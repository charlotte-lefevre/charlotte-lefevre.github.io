Charlotte Lefevre

I am a PhD student in the Digital Security Group at Radboud University Nijmegen since September 2021, under the supervision of Bart Mennink and with Joan Daemen as promotor. During my PhD, I had the opportunity to complete a 6-month internship at CSEM, under the supervision of Damian Vizár. Before that, I completed my Master's thesis at the University of Grenoble under the supervision of Pierre Karpman.

Most of my current research focuses on the provable security of symmetric cryptographic schemes, particularly permutation-based ones.
